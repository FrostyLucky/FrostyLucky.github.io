Hidden Knot

LeBron 17's, LeBron james

This Knot is has become more and more popular over the years as shoes develop in design. It is mostly used in shoes like these LeBrons that have a high flap which can hide the laces. This style is done by doing the basic knot as shown before. And then continuing by pulling the knot under the flap.

Double Knot

Air Force 1, ,Nike

This knot is very simple to do and does not look much different from a regualar shoe knot. This is done by doing the regualar knot and then tying it again. This form is very beneificial because it prevents the knot from opening easily.
